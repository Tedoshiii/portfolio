About Teodor

My Name is Teodor Penchev and I am a self-taught freelance concept artist and illustrator. My biggest interest is game art and I love painting characters from different video games. I enjoy working in full color, exploring different methods of storytelling through drawing and bringing the authentic painting feel to my digital illustrations.
